About Department of Financial Services

Empowering India through Financial Inclusion and Economic Progress

Our History

The Department of Financial Services (DFS) was established in 2008, carved out from the Department of Economic Affairs to provide focused attention to the financial sector. This strategic move was aimed at strengthening India's financial architecture and promoting inclusive growth.

Since its inception, DFS has been at the forefront of transformative financial reforms, including the world's largest financial inclusion program - Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana, revolutionary social security schemes, and digital payment infrastructure development.

Our journey reflects India's commitment to ensuring that every citizen, regardless of their economic status or geographic location, has access to formal financial services and can participate in the nation's economic growth story.
