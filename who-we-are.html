Who We Are

Department of Financial Services - Driving Financial Inclusion and Economic Growth

About the Department

The Department of Financial Services (DFS) is responsible for policy formulation and implementation in the financial sector, particularly focusing on banking, insurance, and pension reforms in India. Established as a separate department in 2008, DFS plays a crucial role in ensuring financial stability and promoting inclusive growth.

We oversee the functioning of banks, financial institutions, and insurance companies, ensuring they align with national priorities of financial inclusion, digital empowerment, and sustainable economic development.

Our initiatives have transformed India's financial landscape, bringing millions into the formal financial system through innovative schemes like Jan Dhan Yojana, social security schemes, and digital payment infrastructure.

Department of Financial Services, New Delhi

Our Vision

To build a financially inclusive society where every citizen has access to formal financial services, enabling them to participate in and benefit from India's economic growth, while ensuring the stability and efficiency of the financial system.

Our Mission

  • Ensure universal access to financial services
  • Strengthen the banking and insurance sectors
  • Promote digital financial inclusion
  • Enhance financial literacy and consumer protection
  • Foster innovation in financial technology

Our Core Values
