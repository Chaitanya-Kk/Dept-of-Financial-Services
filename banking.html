Overview

The Banking Division of the Department of Financial Services is responsible for policy formulation and implementation in the banking sector. We oversee the functioning of Public Sector Banks (PSBs), monitor their performance, and ensure financial inclusion through various government schemes.

Our primary objectives include strengthening the banking system, improving credit flow to productive sectors, ensuring customer protection, and promoting digital banking initiatives across the country.
